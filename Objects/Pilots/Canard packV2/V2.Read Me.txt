NOTE: This is the second release of my canard pack, the names of the canards should be correct now.



This is a pack containing the 3D models for the Canards from my F-16XF Gyrfalcon.
There are individual models and skins for the left and right canard canted at positve 15 degrees, 
0 degrees horizontal and negative 15 degrees. 

To get you started here are the relavent lines from the data.ini of the example F-4C

//Add the following to the relavent system, e.g. nose, fusilage, canopy, etc, replacing the numbers
with suitable ones

SystemName[005]=Canardleftup
SystemName[006]=Canardleft
SystemName[007]=Canardleftdown
SystemName[008]=Canardrightup
SystemName[009]=Canardright
SystemName[010]=Canardrightdown
