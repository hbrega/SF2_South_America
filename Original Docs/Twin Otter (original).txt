 - FEATURES:

Animation key 1 = Pilot Door open/closed (Internal and external animation)

Animation key 2 = Copilot Door open/closed (Internal and external animation)

Animation Key 3 = Cargo bay door open/close

CTRL+O (weapon bay door) = Passengers stairs down/up

Capability of loading 15 paratroopers, launched as a bomb (experimental)

External and internal lights (passengers cabin)


 - FLIGHT MODEL:

Take Off = Flaps 1. Roll untill 75 knots. 15º nose up. Flaps up over 100 knots. Landing gear fixed.

Landing = Flaps 2. 80-85 knots aproximation from 1000-1200 feet AGL, 3-5 miles ayway from runway.

Maneuvering:

		Max operative G = 1.5

		Max structural G = 2.0 (DO NOT EXCEED!)

		Vmax = 160 knots

		Vcruise = 140 knots

		Vstall = 70-75 knots

Range ferry = 1750 km

Operative range = 900 km

Max cargo load = 1700 kg

In service since - until = 1969 - 2020
